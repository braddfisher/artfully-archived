Good to know

What counts as one item?

A single piece of paper is one item. A multi-page stapled report is one project. A bound notebook is priced separately.

Are originals returned?

Yes. Artwork is handled carefully and returned after scanning or photography.

What file formats are included?

Most archives include high-resolution images, organized folders, and a PDF album when included in the package.

Can schools or daycares order?

Yes. Institutional collections can be quoted separately, especially at 500+ items.
